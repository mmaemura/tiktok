♫ Our Goal ♪

Over the past several years, the social media app “TikTok” has gained substantial popularity, and according to Cloudflare, was the most visited website in 2021 surpassing Google. In popular culture, TikTok is regarded as an addictive app and many users report spending hours scrolling through videos on TikTok. Since social media addiction is associated with anxiety and depression, we hoped to closely examine the content posted on the app and the effect that it may have on its users. To do so, we collected text data from trending TikToks including captions, hashtags, and sounds to analyze their sentiments using sentiment analysis.

♫ What is Sentiment Analysis? ♪

Sentiment analysis is a form of natural language processing (NLP) in which the computer is able to detect the emotional tone inferred from the text. In our project, we use the VADER sentiment analyzer that was specifically built to analyze the sentiment of text used in social media.
